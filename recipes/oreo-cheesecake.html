Oreo Cheesecake

Easy to prepare and a great dessert

This Oreo cheesecake is smooth, rich, and full of indulgent cookies and creme flavor.

Ingredients

  • Oreos
  • Butter
  • Cream cheese
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Crush 16 cookies and make the crust.
  2. Make the filling, then mix in half of the remaining chopped cookies.
  3. Pour the filling into the crust and sprinkle with the remaining chopped cookies.
  4. Bake until the center is set. Refrigerate until the cheesecake is completely set.
